Overview

The MSP430 family of microcontrollers from Texas Instruments offers ultra-low-power performance and advanced capabilities for a wide range of applications.

Features

  • Low power consumption
  • High performance
  • Wide range of peripherals
  • Easy development

Applications

MSP430 microcontrollers are ideal for applications such as:

  • Wearables
  • Home automation
  • Industrial control
  • Healthcare devices
